# Interactive Technical Workshop

Iteratively refine ideas through structured questioning.

## Process

1. Clarify the technical domain: architecture, algorithms, data modeling, API design, infrastructure, or another area.
2. Gather constraints: scale, data volume, throughput, stack, dependencies, compliance, latency, cost, timeline, and team size.
3. Present two to four approaches with tradeoffs:
   - Simplest viable option
   - Scalable but complex option
   - Middle ground
   - Risky but high-reward option, when applicable
4. After user chooses a direction, deep dive into concerns, unknowns, and edge cases.
5. Recommend next steps: prototype target, reading/research, and decision checkpoints.

## Rules

- Do not commit to one solution in the first two steps.
- Present tradeoffs, not just options.
- Ask before assuming technical constraints.
- Keep questions short and focused.
- Summarize understanding before moving phases.
